Announcement for Open APIO 2015

For those who are not participating in APIO 2015, you can join Open APIO 2015. Details are shown below.

What is APIO 2015?
Open APIO 2015 is unofficial version of APIO 2015, and open for public, so everybody (especially curious ones) can see and try APIO 2015 problems. Open APIO 2015 will be held on Sunday, 10 May 2015.

How can I join Open APIO 2015?
The registration for Open APIO 2015 is open now in this site: https://competition.ia-toki.org/contests/18/view. If you don't have TLX account, you will be prompted to register. After that, you must register to APIO 2015 - Public Unofficial contest, and you have successfully joined Open APIO 2015.


When is Open APIO 2015?
Open APIO 2015 schedule can be seen here in the schedules. It should be right after the end of whole contest period of APIO 2015.

We hope that you can participate in Open APIO 2015.

APIO 2015 Regulations Changes

Due to a timezone problem, we have expanded the competition time range. The new one is GMT+0 11.00, May 8 - 11.00, May 10, 2015 (it's 48 hours).

We also expanded the maximum contestants per site to 100 instead of 60, so every delegation can have up to 600 hundred contestants.

Please refer to Regulations Page for more information.
