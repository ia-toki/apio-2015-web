APIO 2015 Call for Tasks

We are looking for tasks for APIO 2015 which will be held on 9th May 2015. You may submit your proposal between now and 18th April 2015. Your contribution is highly appreciated. Please click here to download APIO 2015 - Call For Tasks Guides. Your contribution is highly appreciated.

APIO 2015 Important Dates

Important dates related to APIO 2015 are as follows.

  • Call for tasks deadline: 18th April (Sat)
  • Draft tasks-set distribution: 25th April (Sat)
  • Tasks-set voting deadline: 30th April (Thu)
  • Final tasks-set distribution: 2nd May (Sat)
  • Tasks-set translation deadline: 7th May (Thu)
  • Competition day: 9th May (Sat)
